Follow these simple guidelines to help your plants flourish and create a thriving indoor garden

01

Water Regularly

Keep your plants hydrated with the right amount of water. Each species has unique needs, so check the soil moisture before watering.

02

Provide Light

Most plants need adequate sunlight to thrive. Place them near windows or use grow lights for indoor plants in low-light areas.

03

Ensure Airflow

Good air circulation prevents diseases and promotes healthy growth. Avoid overcrowding and ensure proper ventilation.
